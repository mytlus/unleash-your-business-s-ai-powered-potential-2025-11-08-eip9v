About Twenty One AI

Founded in 2019 by a team of AI researchers and business automation experts, Twenty One AI was born from a simple vision: to democratize artificial intelligence and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes. Our founders recognized that while large enterprises had resources to build custom AI solutions, most organizations lacked the expertise and capital to harness this transformative technology. We set out to change that by creating an agency that combines cutting-edge AI research with practical business acumen, delivering enterprise-grade automation solutions at scale.

Today, Twenty One AI stands as a beacon of innovation in the automation industry. Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI automation solutions and services.

How long does it take to implement an AI automation solution?

The implementation timeline depends on the complexity and scope of your project. Simple RPA solutions typically take 4-8 weeks from discovery to deployment, while more complex AI workflows may take 3-6 months. This includes discovery, design, development, testing, training, and full deployment. We work in agile sprints to deliver value incrementally, so you can see benefits early in the process. Our team will provide a detailed project timeline during the initial consultation.

Will AI automation replace our employees?

Absolutely not. Our philosophy is to augment human capabilities, not replace them. AI automation handles repetitive, rule-based tasks, freeing your team to focus on strategic, creative, and customer-facing work that requires human judgment and expertise. In fact, most of our clients report that automation leads to increased job satisfaction as employees move away from mundane tasks. We also provide comprehensive change management and training to help your team transition smoothly to their new roles.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

Data security is paramount in everything we do. Our solutions are built with enterprise-grade security standards including encryption, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit trails. We comply with major regulations including GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud platforms with regular security assessments and penetration testing. We also work closely with your IT and compliance teams to ensure all solutions meet your specific regulatory requirements and security policies.

What kind of ROI can we expect?

Most of our clients achieve ROI within 6-12 months of implementation. The exact ROI depends on your specific use case, but we typically see 30-40% reduction in operational costs, 50-70% reduction in processing time, and significant improvements in accuracy and compliance. During the initial discovery phase, we conduct a thorough cost-benefit analysis to provide realistic projections for your business. We also track KPIs throughout implementation to ensure you're achieving the expected benefits and can optimize the solution as needed.

What ongoing support and maintenance do you provide?

We provide comprehensive ongoing support through our managed services offerings. This includes 24/7 monitoring, regular maintenance updates, performance optimization, and access to our support team for troubleshooting and enhancements. We also offer training programs to keep your team updated on best practices and new features. Our solutions are designed to evolve with your business - as your needs change, we can quickly adapt and enhance your automation workflows. Most clients opt for our premium support package which includes quarterly business reviews and continuous improvement recommendations.
